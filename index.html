About Me

Hi! Im Katie and I am a UX designer who’s goal is to solve problems from an empathetic and human driven mindset

My approach to design is to use simple and intuitive methods to make people’s everyday lives a little easier. I also try and incorporate a little fun and whimsy into daily life to spark a bit of joy.

What sets me apart from other designers is that I come from a research background. I solve problems by gathering data directly from people and forming ideas and conclusions that directly reflect the needs of the users.
